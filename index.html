Student Organizations of Bangladesh Dataset (ZPDP-SOBD)

This dataset is a comprehensive resource detailing student organizations in Bangladesh. It includes data from major student unions, their activities, and historical significance. Developed with contributions from Team Karigor, JRC Computer Society, and ZNRF University of Management Sciences.
